Traffic areas

Great Britain is divided into 8 traffic areas. Each area has a Traffic Commissioner who’s responsible for issuing licences. Northern Ireland has a eparate system.

Your operating licence needs to be valid for the area(s) in which your operating centre(s) are based. If you have operating centres in more than one traffic area, you will need a separate licence for each area.
