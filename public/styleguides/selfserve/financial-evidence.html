Financial evidence

To be granted a licence, you’ll need to show you have enough money to keep your vehicles and run your business. Read more about financial evidence

How much will you need?

£12,998

This figure’s based on:

  • an application for 20 vehicles
  • maintenance of 19 vehicles on your other licences

Send a copy to us

Once you have your financial evidence, you should either upload or post a copy to us. See the types of evidence we accept.

How would you like to send your evidence?

  • PDF, DOC, JPG, PNG or GIF

If you’d rather post a copy, send it as soon as possible to:

Office of the Traffic Commissioner
The Central Licensing Office
Hillcrest House
Leeds, LS9 6NF

Make sure you include your application reference: OB1523424/133.
