Bus registrations

Apply online

You can manage and view your bus services online through the electronic bus service registration (EBSR) service.

There are certain I.T requirements that you'll need to meet before you can use EBSR.

Email ebsr@vosa.gov.uk or call 0113 254 3200 for more information.
