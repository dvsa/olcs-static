Register, change or cancel bus services

You can apply to register, change or cancel bus services online through the electronic bus service registration (EBSR) service.

If you haven't done this before you'll need to email ebsr@vosa.gov.uk or call 0113 254 3200.

You can also register, change or cancel bus services using the paper forms.

Before you upload

  • Make sure the XML document follows the TransXchange standard
  • Compress the XML document and any supporting files such as maps, timetables and letters of support into a single .ZIP file
