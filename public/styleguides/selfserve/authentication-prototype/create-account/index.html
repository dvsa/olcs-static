Vehicle Operator Licence service

Apply for, check and update goods or public service vehicle (PSV) operator’s licences online. You’ll need to create an account first before you can start using the service.

This service replaces the VOSA Self Service system. You can still log in to your existing Self Service account using your current username and password through the new service.

Start now

Before you start

Before you create an account and apply for your first licence, ensure that you have read up on the legal obligations of being either a goods or public service vehicle operator.

Once you have created an account you’ll be sent a temporary password to your email address. You can then start using the online service.


What you can do

You can:

  • update your existing licence and application details
  • apply for new licences
  • check the status of your current applications
  • pay licence fees
  • add or remove vehicles
  • order duplicate operator licence discs
  • manage your bus registrations (PSV only)
