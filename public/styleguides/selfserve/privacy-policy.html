Privacy policy

Data protection

The Traffic Commissioner (or the DOE in Northern Ireland) will use your personal information as part of their work.

They may share it with:

  • other Traffic Commissioners
  • the Department for Transport
  • other government departments
  • enforcement agencies

We might also contact you about user research.

If you apply for a standard licence we’ll add your details to a national register.

IP addresses

We use IP addresses to:

  • monitor trends
  • see which pages users visit
  • learn how different sections of the population use the site

We can’t use your IP address to identify you personally.

Cookies

Our website puts small files called cookies on to your computer.

Cookies collect information about how you browse the site. They can’t be used to identify you personally.

We use them to:

  • measure how you use the site so we can update and improve it
  • remember notifications you’ve seen so we don’t show them to you again

Find out more about how to manage cookies.

Most services we link to are run by different government departments like HMRC or the Home Office.

These services may set other cookies and will have their own cookie policies.
