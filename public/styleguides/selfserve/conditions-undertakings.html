Conditions and undertakings

To change your conditions or undertakings email: operatorlicensing@otc.gov.uk.

Licence conditions

  • send bank statements covering the past three months to the Traffic Commissioner
  • carry out a full safety inspection on all your vehicles and trailers every six weeks

Operating centre conditions

  • not weigh more than 7.5 tonnes
  • enter and exit in forward gear
  • be parked off-street when they’re not being used

You must also follow the conditions you agreed to in the declaration.
